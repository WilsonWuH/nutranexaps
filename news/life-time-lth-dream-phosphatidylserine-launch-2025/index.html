Life Time Uses PS in Melatonin-Free Sleep Formula

By Nutranexa News | July 15, 2026

Life Time's new LTH Dream formula includes phosphatidylserine alongside magnesium, pharmaGABA, and other sleep-support ingredients in a melatonin-free format.

What Launched

Life Time announced on August 12, 2025 that it was launching LTH Dream, a melatonin-free sleep formula backed by clinical research messaging.

The release listed phosphatidylserine with magnesium glycinate, pharmaGABA, and other ingredients, showing PS in another stress-and-sleep adjacent blend rather than a standalone cognition concept.

Formulation Signal

PS appearing in sleep products matters to ingredient buyers because it broadens the range of applications where phospholipid ingredients must work inside multi-active systems.

That raises more practical questions on taste, stability, moisture control, and how the ingredient behaves alongside calming actives in different delivery formats.

Buyer Takeaway

Suppliers serving this type of concept should expect more requests around compatibility, handling, and claim discipline than around source identity alone.

A launch like this is best read as a formulation-direction signal for cognitive-nutrition ingredients, not as automatic evidence for every market's sleep claims.
