Sports Research Pushes Krill Oil Line Deeper Into Costco

By Nutranexa News | July 22, 2026

Sports Research says its Costco footprint now covers 10 wellness SKUs, including a krill-oil line built around phospholipid-bound omega-3s, choline, and sourcing transparency.

What Expanded

Sports Research said on December 18, 2025 that it now offers ten nutritional supplements at Costco warehouses nationwide, with eight products added during 2025.

The company described the growth as its largest footprint in the warehouse channel.

Why the Krill Line Matters

Among the highlighted products, the release singled out SUPERBA2 krill oil with phospholipid-bound omega-3s, choline, and astaxanthin, plus scan-to-source transparency messaging.

That puts phospholipid-delivered omega-3s into a broader retail-scale distribution story rather than a niche specialty launch.

Buyer Takeaway

For ingredient buyers, the expansion is a reminder that warehouse retail still rewards sourcing clarity, stable supply, and a sharp technical story when premium lipids move into high-volume channels.

Suppliers watching phospholipid demand should track not only launches but also how far those formulations spread across mainstream retail assortments.
