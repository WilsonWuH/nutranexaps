What Was Announced

Kyowa Hakko USA announced on June 9, 2026 that Wellex had launched Creatine Well, a women's supplement formulated with Cognizin citicoline, Pürest Creatine and Sharp-PS phosphatidylserine.

Formula and Format

According to the announcement, the product is an unflavored powder with no sweeteners, flavors, caffeine or fillers. The release positions the three-ingredient formula around cognition, strength, recovery and healthy aging.

Relevance for Ingredient Buyers

The launch is a current example of phosphatidylserine being used in a multi-ingredient powder rather than only in tablets or capsules. Buyers evaluating similar concepts still need to confirm source, assay, carrier, powder behavior, serving size and permitted market claims for the finished product.
