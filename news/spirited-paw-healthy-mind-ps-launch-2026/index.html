Spirited Paw Adds PS to Canine Cognitive Supplement Launch

By Nutranexa News | July 17, 2026

Standard Process says its Spirited Paw Healthy Mind launch combines phosphatidylserine with B vitamins, vitamin E, and MCT oil, extending PS use into aging-dog cognitive supplements.

What Entered The Market

Standard Process introduced Spirited Paw Healthy Mind on March 9, 2026 as a brain-health supplement for aging dogs showing cognitive and behavioral changes.

The company said the formula includes phosphatidylserine, essential B vitamins, vitamin E, and organic medium-chain triglycerides alongside farm-sourced whole-food ingredients.

Why It Matters

For PS suppliers, the launch is another sign that cognitive-nutrition ingredients are crossing species categories and appearing in premium pet-health formats, not only human capsules and powders.

That can broaden interest in source options, oxidation control, palatability support, and specification handling for pet-nutrition customers.

Buyer Takeaway

Suppliers serving companion-animal markets should treat this as a formulation and documentation signal rather than a blanket efficacy proof for PS in pets.

Buyers evaluating similar concepts will still need to review pet-market compliance, ingredient handling, and the practicality of delivering PS in the intended dosage form.
