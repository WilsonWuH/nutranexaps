AddiVance Launch Highlights PS in Family Focus Formula

By Nutranexa News | July 10, 2026

Medella Springs says its new AddiVance formula is positioned for daily focus and attention support, adding another current phosphatidylserine use case in family-oriented supplement marketing.

What Launched

Medella Springs announced AddiVance on June 1, 2026 as a drug-free option positioned for daily focus, attention, and cognitive support.

The release adds another current example of phosphatidylserine appearing in routine cognitive-support formulas rather than only in memory-specific products.

How PS Was Positioned

The company framed the product around families navigating attention and focus concerns, showing that PS continues to be used in consumer concepts that sit between cognition, learning routines, and everyday performance.

That positioning matters for ingredient buyers because it broadens the commercial contexts where PS dosage, taste-masking, and cost-in-use need to work.

Buyer Takeaway

Suppliers serving this type of launch need to support clean source documentation, age-appropriate formulation planning, and a realistic cost structure at consumer serving sizes.

Buyers should also separate ingredient facts from retail messaging and review market-specific claim boundaries before adapting similar concepts.
