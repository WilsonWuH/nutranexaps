SmartyPants Adds 480 mg Choline to New Prenatal Capsule Launch

By Nutranexa News | July 22, 2026

SmartyPants says its new Prenatal Plus Multivitamin Capsules deliver 480 mg of choline per daily serving, giving buyers another current read on how prenatal brands are increasing choline inclusion.

What Launched

SmartyPants announced its Prenatal Plus Multivitamin Capsules on March 16, 2026 as a new OB-GYN co-developed formula positioned for preconception and pregnancy support.

The release says the product will be available on Amazon first and in select Target locations from April.

Why Buyers Watch It

The most relevant formulation detail for ingredient suppliers is the stated 480 mg of choline per daily serving, alongside methylfolate, iron, and a modest DHA inclusion.

The launch also shows how prenatal brands are moving beyond token choline levels and turning nutrient amount into a visible commercial differentiator.

Buyer Takeaway

Choline suppliers serving prenatal accounts should expect sharper questions on dose density, capsule burden, taste and odor control, and third-party testing support.

Commercial teams should separate the brand's marketing language from what their own technical files can support in each market.
