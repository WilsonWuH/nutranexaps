Bobbie Expands Whole-Milk Formula with MFGM and Choline

By Nutranexa News | July 17, 2026

What Rolled Out

Bobbie said its updated 100% organic whole-milk infant formula began rolling out across roughly 1,900 Target stores from mid-March 2026.

The company described the revised formula as supporting brain development with MFGM, DHA, and choline while shifting from 98% to 100% whole milk content.

Why Ingredient Buyers Watch It

For suppliers, the notable point is not just the retail expansion. It is the continued use of phospholipid-rich MFGM and choline as part of the premium formula architecture being marketed at scale.

That reinforces demand for ingredients that can fit infant-nutrition quality expectations while supporting stable supply, controlled specifications, and region-specific documentation.

Buyer Takeaway

Ingredient teams serving early-life nutrition should watch which lipid systems are moving from specialist discussion into mainstream shelf expansion.

Commercial relevance depends on more than inclusion claims: buyers still need to confirm source route, application fit, contaminant controls, and the regulatory status of each component in the destination market.
