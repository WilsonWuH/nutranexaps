Midi Health Debuts Women 40+ Supplement Line With PS Formula

What Entered the Market

Midi Health announced on August 6, 2025 that it was launching its first supplement line tailored to women 40 and older.

The launch included a Daily Cortisol Support formula that uses phosphatidylserine within a broader women's midlife wellness concept.

Why It Matters

This is a useful market signal because it places PS in a hormone- and stress-adjacent supplement story rather than a classic memory-only pitch.

That kind of crossover positioning can change formulation priorities around flavor systems, serving format, and how PS is combined with botanicals or other actives.

Buyer Takeaway

Ingredient teams following women's health launches should watch where PS is being grouped with stress, aging, or hormonal-support narratives and whether those combinations affect source choice or target assay.

As always, buyers need to separate company marketing language from what their own markets permit on-pack or in technical sales material.
