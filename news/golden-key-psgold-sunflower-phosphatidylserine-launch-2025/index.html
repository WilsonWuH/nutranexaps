Golden Key Launches Sunflower-Derived PSgold Ingredient

What Launched

Golden Key Ingredients said on July 1, 2025 that it was introducing PSgold, a sunflower-derived phosphatidylserine in powdered form.

The company positioned the ingredient for capsule, tablet, and powdered-mix applications, making the launch directly relevant to formulation teams that need a non-soy PS option.

Why It Matters

Supplier-side launches matter because they can expand source options in a category where buyers often balance allergen positioning, cost-in-use, and finished-format behavior at the same time.

A sunflower-derived powdered PS also keeps attention on flowability, carrier choice, oxidation control, and spec consistency rather than source language alone.

Buyer Takeaway

Buyers comparing new PS offers should request assay method, carrier disclosure, particle behavior, and the usual quality pack before treating a launch announcement as a qualified supply option.

The strongest commercial fit will depend on how well the powder performs in the intended delivery system, not just on a sunflower-origin claim.
