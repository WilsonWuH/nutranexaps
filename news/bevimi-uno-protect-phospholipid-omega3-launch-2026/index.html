BEVIMI Launches Liquid Brain Formula Built Around Phospholipid Omega-3s

By Nutranexa News | July 22, 2026

BEVIMI's new Uno Protect launch packages phospholipid-form omega-3s from Norwegian herring roe into a shelf-stable liquid shot, signaling continued premium interest in complex brain-lipid systems.

What Launched

BEVIMI introduced Uno Protect on May 13, 2026 as a daily liquid brain-health formulation built around ten clinically studied actives in a 1.7-ounce serving.

The company says the formula begins with a soft launch and broader rollout through 2026.

Formulation Signal

The release highlights phospholipid-form DHA and EPA from Norwegian herring roe, additional phospholipid complex from herring roe, and a shelf-stable liquid system for hydrophobic bioactives.

BEVIMI also says an IRB-approved randomized, double-blind, placebo-controlled study is planned around biomarker responses relevant to brain health prevention and cognitive aging.

Buyer Takeaway

For phospholipid ingredient suppliers, the notable point is the attempt to push premium brain-lipid systems into fully disclosed liquid delivery rather than another capsule stack.

That raises practical sourcing and stability questions around lipid form, oxidation control, packaging, and cost structure before similar concepts can scale.
